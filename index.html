Argentina

Argentina is a large country in South America, known for its diverse landscapes, rich culture, and vibrant cities. It spans from the tropical north to the icy wilderness of Patagonia in the south, making it one of the most geographically diverse countries in the world. The Andes mountains run along its western border with Chile, while the Atlantic Ocean lies to the east. The city of Mar del Plata is one of the most famous cities in Argentina, is a very turistic city where people from all around Argentina and the world visit on summer, due to the beaches and the activities this city provides.
