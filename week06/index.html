History of Buenos Aires

Buenos Aires, the vibrant capital of Argentina, was founded in 1536 by Pedro de Mendoza. Known for its rich cultural heritage, the city blends European architecture with Latin American spirit. Over centuries, Buenos Aires has become a hub for art, music, and gastronomy, offering visitors an unforgettable experience.

Top Activities

  • Explore La Boca: Visit the colorful Caminito street and enjoy tango performances.
  • Walk in Recoleta: Stroll through the famous Recoleta Cemetery and nearby parks.
  • Savor Argentine Cuisine: Try world-class steaks at a parrilla or enjoy empanadas.
  • Discover Palermo: Shop, eat, and relax in trendy Palermo's cafes and boutiques.
  • Experience Tango: Attend a tango show or take a beginner's lesson.
