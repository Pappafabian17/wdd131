Site Plan Document

Site Name

Name: Where To Go Buenos Aires

This name was selected because it highlights the core function of the website: helping users discover and plan their experiences in Buenos Aires. It's simple, clear, and invites visitors to explore their options in the city.

Domain: where2goBSAS.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to help travelers plan their vacations to Buenos Aires efficiently by providing:

  • A list of the best places to visit, from historical landmarks to trendy neighborhoods.
  • Recommendations for activities, including cultural experiences, nightlife, and outdoor adventures.
  • Travel tips, such as transportation options and local customs.
  • Itineraries optimized for different trip lengths and preferences.

Scenarios

  • What are the must-see landmarks in Buenos Aires?
  • Where can I find the best tango shows?
  • Are there any family-friendly activities in the city?
  • What is the best way to get around Buenos Aires?

Color Schema

Primary Color (#3c6604): Used for headings and buttons.
Dark Primary Color (#144517): Used for footer backgrounds or accents.
Accent Color (#4CAF50): Used for links and call-to-action buttons.
Text Color (#212121): Used for primary text.
Secondary Text Color (#757575): Used for subtitles or less prominent text.
Divider Color (#92A28C): Used for borders or separators.

Typography

The chosen typography for the website is:

  • Font: Open Sans (Google Fonts)
  • Usage:
    • Headings: Bold Open Sans
    • Body Text: Regular Open Sans
    • Captions or small text: Light Open Sans
